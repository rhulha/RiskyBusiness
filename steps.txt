RISK - Player Turn Steps
========================

1. REINFORCE
   - Count territories owned: floor(territories / 3), minimum 3
   - Add continent bonuses for any continent you fully control:
       North America  +5
       South America  +2
       Europe         +5
       Africa         +3
       Asia           +7
       Australia      +2
   - Place all earned armies on your own territories (one at a time)

2. ATTACK  (optional, repeat as many times as you like)
   - Choose one of your territories with 2+ armies as the attacker
   - Choose an adjacent enemy territory as the target
   - Roll dice:
       Attacker rolls up to 3 dice (max = attacking armies - 1)
       Defender rolls up to 2 dice (max = defending armies)
   - Compare highest dice pairs; lower value loses 1 army (ties go to defender)
   - If defender reaches 0 armies: territory is captured
       Move at least as many armies as dice you rolled into the new territory
   - Continue attacking or stop at any time

3. FORTIFY  (once per turn, optional)
   - Move any number of armies (leaving at least 1 behind) from one of your
     territories to one connected friendly territory
   - Connection can be through any chain of territories you own
   - Skip if you don't want to move armies

4. END TURN
   - Pass to the next player
